Alfredo

Best Alfredo suace

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Warm up the butter and crean in a large saucepan over low heat
  2. Let it simmer for 2 minutes
  3. Whisk the garlic and Seasonings
  4. Add the Parmesan cheese and stir just until the cheese is melted
